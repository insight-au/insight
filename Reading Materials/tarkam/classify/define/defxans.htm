1. Shadow:
Absolute negation of light is only darkness and not shadow.  So it is right to define shadow as the region of reduced intensity of light (in comparison with its surroundings). It is interesting to note that there is dependence of shadow on its object and a shadow if present is always associated with an object (be it even fire, i.e., you can even get the shadow of fire in the presence of a high power torch.).

2.Garden:
"A place where plants and trees (earth) are grown(collectively)"
Different from naturally growing forest. To be precise "a place where plants or trees are grown for to cater to one's domestic needs".

  • Dravya : Earth
  • Guna   : samyoga (Conjunction of objects such as plants and trees)

3. Juice:
"Liquid that can be consumed, having an agreeable taste(quality)".

  • Dravya : Water
  • Guna   : Taste (property of water)

4.Mirror:
"A solid Substance(earth) that shows the object whose light(fire) falls on it and which doesn't allow light to pass through".  This clearly  states that it is different from water which also shows ones reflections.

  • Dravya : Earth
  • Guna   : Conjunction of Earth and Fire to produce image in the mirror

5.Paper:
Thin, flexible, processed sheets made from wood by man that can be used for writing and packaging.

  • Dravya : Earth
  • Guna   : Properties of earth.
